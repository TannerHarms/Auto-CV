# Projects

## There and Back Again — Archival & Editorial Project

Comprehensive archival edition of Bilbo Baggins' travel memoir, including cross-referenced maps and annotations.
**Technologies:** Manuscript restoration, Tengwar transcription, cartographic illustration

- Organised 300+ pages of handwritten notes into chronological narrative
- Added 40 margin annotations with Elvish translations and historical context
- Capstone research project — praised by Gandalf the Grey as "uncommonly thorough"

## Bag End Library Catalogue

Complete inventory and classification system for the Baggins family collection of maps, books, and artefacts.
**Technologies:** Hobbit cataloguing standards, Elvish cross-indexing, archival preservation

- Catalogued 450+ volumes spanning Shire genealogy, Elvish poetry, and Dwarven trade records
- Developed custom indexing system linking related texts across 3 languages

## Shire Boundary Survey

Volunteer mapping project documenting paths, waterways, and landmarks across the four Farthings.
**Technologies:** Cartography, field survey, compass navigation

- Surveyed 120+ miles of footpaths and produced 8 detailed regional maps
