# Projects

## Operation Conspiracy — Covert Departure Support

Full-scope covert operation to support Frodo Baggins' departure from the Shire, including intelligence gathering, logistics, and route planning.
**Technologies:** Cartographic analysis, covert communication, safe-house procurement

- Recruited and managed 3 operatives (Sam Gamgee, Pippin Took, Fredegar Bolger)
- Identified and mapped 2 alternative escape routes through Buckland and the Old Forest
- Maintained cover story for 12 months while gathering intelligence on Ring-wraith movements

## Buckland Emergency Response Plan

Comprehensive emergency preparedness framework for the Buckland region covering Old Forest incursions, flooding, and external threats.
**Technologies:** Risk assessment, evacuation mapping, resource allocation modelling

- Catalogued 15 threat scenarios with response protocols and resource requirements
- Established alarm relay system connecting Brandy Hall to 8 outlying settlements
- Plan adopted as official Buckland defence doctrine by the Master of Buckland

## Old Forest Path Survey

Multi-year cartographic project documenting safe paths through the Old Forest east of the Hedge.
**Technologies:** Field survey, compass triangulation, seasonal observation logging

- Mapped 6 viable paths with difficulty ratings and seasonal closure periods
- Documented hazardous areas including Withywindle valley and Old Man Willow territory
- First comprehensive Old Forest survey in 200+ years
